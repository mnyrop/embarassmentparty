/ Viktor Timofeev /

Viktor Timofeev gets excited about many things, including new mediums/ possibilities/ experiences afforded by new technologies, inventions and conventions, while others include immersive/ imaginative fiction, intense/ alienating/ psychedelic experiences, work that has a strong sense of heart, long discussions particularly with people that he doesn't immediately see eye to eye with. Viktor used to study programming and was on a road to a menial job in network admin when he discovered he was very much into drawing. After 7 years of drawing, he has rediscovered programming and is presently trying to negotiate that relationship. Viktor's recent work has taken the form of songs, websites, drawings and a collaborative science-fiction story written with his little brother in over a year's worth of emails.
